What is Rheumatoid Arthritis(RA)?

Rheumatoid arthritis, also known as RA, is a chronic autoimunne disease that affects mostly joints. Like any other autoimunne diseases, it cannot be cured, however it can be treated with surgery, physiotherapy and through self-care.

    Symptoms include:
  • Skin Symptoms
    1. Lumps
    2. Redness
  • Joint Symptoms
    1. Stiffness
    2. Swelling
    3. Tenderness
  • Also other common Symptoms
    1. Back pain
    2. Fatigue
    3. Dry Mouth
    4. Muscle pain
