Equipment

It is extremly important to have the necessary medical equipment for unexpected joint pains, especially in winter. The table describes the purpose of each medical equipment and where to find them.
Equipment/Device Purpose
Knee Brace It is a medical devive that helps supports an painful or injured knee.
Electric massager Relaxes muscles because of overworked joints.
Cooling pad Used in summer to reduce inflammation and swelling due to extreme heat.
Heating blanket Used in winter to reduce inflammation and swelling due to extreme cold.
