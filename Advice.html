Advice

It is important to understand what your limitations are - such as foods and exercise - when diagnosed.

Foods

Try to not to consume the following foods in big or normal portions:
  • Dairy
  • Sugary foods
  • Carbohydrates
These foods will cause inflammation and swelling, causing muscles and epecially joints to be painful. Try to consume the following foods in big portions
  • Protein - eggs, chickpea and chicken
  • Tumeric
  • Ginger
  • And even vegetables

Unwanted situations

RA does not only affect the joints but also other systems such as the breathing and cardiovascular systems. During this time, try not to put yourself in danger. To elaborate do not do excess exercising, doing of chores, climbing, carry heavy objeacts and especially do not put your self in scary situations.
It is also reccomended to keep surfaces clean and try to avoid becoming sick- catching a cold or having the flu-as it can greatly impact your breathing.

Exercise

Do exercises that work at the joints and try also to gain muscles.
    Recommended exercises:
  • Weight training
  • Cycling
  • Pilates
  • Finger exercising
