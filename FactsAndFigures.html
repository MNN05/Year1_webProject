Facts and Figures in RSA

Given the data collected in 2022 for South Africa, 0.07% in rural areas have RA, whilst 2.5% in urban areas have RA.

The UK, Trinadad and Tobago as well as Barbados, are the countries with the most people with RA.

RA can begin at any age, but it is mostly common among adults in their sixities.

The life expectancy of a person with RA, is about 77 years.
